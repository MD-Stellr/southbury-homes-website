About Southbury Homes

Building to a single, unwavering standard.

Southbury Homes creates distinguished residences and communities across the Greater Toronto Area, defined by outstanding craftsmanship, timeless design, and uncompromising quality.

Your Home, Our Care, Our Promise

You are the inspiration behind everything we build. Your lifestyle. Your vision. Your future.

We create exceptional homes and communities defined by outstanding craftsmanship, timeless design, and uncompromising quality. We believe a home should be more than a place to live. It should reflect who you are, support the life you aspire to create, and provide lasting value for generations to come.

Built on a foundation of integrity, accountability, and experience, we approach every home with the same commitment to excellence that has earned the trust of homeowners, partners, and communities alike. From design and construction to customer care, we hold ourselves to the highest standards, empowering our team to deliver exceptional quality, honest communication, and a seamless homeownership experience.

With premium features, thoughtful details, and superior construction standards included as our benchmark, every Southbury home is thoughtfully crafted to deliver enduring beauty, comfort, and peace of mind. Drawing on decades of expertise while embracing innovation, we continue to build homes and communities that inspire confidence today and stand the test of time tomorrow.

Our Lead

The person accountable for it all.

Southbury is led by its CEO and President, Enzo Raponi, who pairs decades of mortgage, real estate, and homebuilding experience with a genuinely client first approach to every home.

Enzo Raponi

CEO & President

As CEO and President of Southbury Homes, Enzo brings more than 25 years of leadership experience in the mortgage and real estate industries. Recognized for his strategic vision, financial expertise, and client focused approach, he has built a career helping individuals and families achieve their homeownership goals while navigating evolving market conditions. Leveraging his extensive knowledge of mortgage financing, housing markets, and residential development, Enzo leads Southbury Homes with a commitment to quality, innovation, and long term value. His unique perspective on the homeownership journey enables him to align thoughtful community planning with exceptional customer experiences. Driven by integrity, excellence, and a passion for building communities, Enzo is focused on delivering homes that homeowners can be proud of for generations to come.

“A home is the foundation of your life's story. We combine market expertise with thoughtful planning so that the moment you look at your new home, you know it was built to last.”
