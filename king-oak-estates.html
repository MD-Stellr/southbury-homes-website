Welcome to King Oak Estates

Only eight will ever call it home.

8 Architecturally refined residences. No more.

King Oak Estates is an exclusive collection of just eight architecturally refined single-family homes in the prestigious Oak Ridges community of Richmond Hill.

Crafted for modern luxury living, each residence showcases a thoughtfully designed open-concept layout, soaring ceilings, and expansive windows that fill the home with natural light. Elegant interiors feature gourmet kitchens with premium finishes, spa-inspired primary suites, and carefully curated details that blend style with everyday comfort.

Set within a nature-rich enclave, King Oak Estates offers residents a rare sense of privacy while remaining close to top-rated schools, scenic trails, parks, and convenient transit. This is a community where tranquility meets accessibility — perfect for families and discerning homeowners alike.

With only eight homes available, King Oak Estates presents a limited and exceptional opportunity to own in one of Oak Ridges’ most desirable locations. Discover a lifestyle defined by sophistication, exclusivity, and natural beauty.

Your Neighbourhood

Where the moraine meets the everyday.

Oak Ridges sits on the crest of the Oak Ridges Moraine — a rare pocket of the GTA where conservation forest, kettle lakes, and quiet streets share an address with excellent schools, golf, and quick connections to everywhere you need to be.

Trails & Conservation

  • Oak Ridges Moraine trail network
  • Jefferson Forest & Bond Lake woodlands
  • Protected greenlands at your doorstep

Lakes & Parks

  • Lake Wilcox Park & waterfront promenade
  • Sunset Beach & kettle-lake shorelines
  • Neighbourhood parks and playgrounds

Golf & Recreation

  • Renowned golf and country clubs nearby
  • Oak Ridges Community Centre & Pool
  • Sports complexes and year-round programs

Schools & Transit

  • Top-rated public and private schools
  • GO Transit and Viva connections
  • Yonge Street shops and daily conveniences
